Why are people becoming vegan?

There has been an increasing amount of vegan diets worldwide, some statistics even citing up to 600% in the United States since 2014. This project will visualize the reasons why people choose to go vegan and include the following cases.

  • Animal Rights
  • Environmental Reasons
  • Health Reasons
  • Other

Sources will be as follows.

  1. Data Source: Study of Current and Former Vegetarians and Vegans
  2. Data Source: Reasons why people go vegan
  3. Why do people become vegans/vegetarians
  4. Why the dietary trend is so popular
